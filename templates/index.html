Body Mass Index (BMI)

Body Mass Index (BMI) is derived from weight and height measurement of a person. It is used to measure body fat and applies to both adult men and women.

BMI is used to categorize a person as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese based on tissue mass (fat) and height.

The tool below will help you know your BMI. Simply enter your weight in pounds (lbs) and height in feet and inches, then click Calculate BMI button. Your BMI results will be shown below.
